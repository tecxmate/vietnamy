#### Adjectives

Knowing adjectives in Vietnamese is a powerful skill. It can make it easy to have a conversation about different topics by simply knowing some key words such as "good", "bad", "right", "wrong" etc. Adjectives are words used to **describe** or **modify** another person or thing in the sentence for example: I am **happy**. Vietnamese is **easy**.

Below is a list of 20 adjectives that you might come across or use very often. The table contains 3 columns (English, Vietnamese, and Audio).
