# Vietnamese Translation

Welcome to our Vietnamese translation page. Here you will be able to find 3 kinds of services:

1. **[automatic translation](vietnamese_automatic_translation.php "Vietnamese automatic translation")**: a machine does the translation for you automatically and for free. But as with any automatic translator, it's not very reliable.
2. **[free translation](vietnamese_free_translation.php "Vietnamese free translation")**: a volunteer will do the translation for you for **free**. However, only short paragpraphs, phrases and words are allowed.
3. **[paid translation](vietnamese_free_translation.php "Vietnamese paid translation")**: a professional translator will do the translation for you. You can request a quote from the translator directly. You can also discuss the price and deadline.
