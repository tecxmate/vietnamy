As Vietnam continues to integrate into the global economy, attract international investment, and expand trade partnerships, the number of tourists, professionals, and businesses coming to Vietnam has grown rapidly — and so has the demand for learning Vietnamese. To meet this growing need, TECXMATE has developed Vietnamy, the first dedicated Vietnamese learning app on the market.

What is Vietnamy?

Vietnamy is a Vietnamese language and culture learning app designed for:

  • Expats living and working in Vietnam
  • Vietnamese diaspora communities around the world
  • International travelers exploring Vietnam
  • Professionals and business partners working with the Vietnamese market

More Than Just a Language App

Vietnamy is built to deliver a learning experience that is natural, practical, engaging, and meaningful — helping learners pick up Vietnamese quickly through a personalized approach tailored to their individual needs.

Unlike traditional methods, Vietnamy combines:

  • Interactive Lessons — A structured curriculum from beginner to advanced
  • Pronunciation Training — Practice all 6 Vietnamese tones with instant feedback
  • Smart Dictionary — Fast lookup with IPA transcription, real-world examples, and multilingual support
  • Spaced Repetition (SRS) — A scientifically-backed algorithm that helps you remember more, longer
  • Gamification — Points, streaks, and rewards to keep you motivated
  • Vietnamese Culture — Go beyond grammar and gain a deeper understanding of the people and culture

Why Vietnamy?

Today, there is no app on the market truly purpose-built for learning Vietnamese. Popular language apps like Duolingo or Rosetta Stone offer only surface-level Vietnamese content, lacking depth in grammar, tones, and cultural context.

Vietnamy was created to fill that gap — a product built from the ground up for Vietnamese, by people who understand the language.
